First Featured Meal. Garden Rainbow Wraps

There's definitely a sushi vibe to these kid-friendly wraps, which are stuffed with vegetables, cheese and hummus, then rolled and sliced. Serve them with store-bought green goddess dressing, a creamy herb-filled dip.

Second Featured Meal. Sizzling Pepper Quesadillas

These 15-minute quesadillas are a notch above basic with the addition of sautéed peppers and onions. Let the kids load them up with toppings at the table.

Third Featured Meal. Garlic Butter Shrimp Linguine

Shrimp scampi is a favorite dish at many Italian restaurants, but this version takes just 20 minutes to prepare, so it's perfect for a weeknight dinner at home. Large shrimp are cooked with garlic and then served over linguine pasta with a buttery wine sauce. It's so good your family may think you ordered takeout.
